My Top 3 Favorite Cartoon Characters and Why

Buttercup

Buttercup is a character from the Powerpuff Girls. She is brave, strong, and has the best personality in my opinion. When I was a kid, I always thought that the other two characters were lame since one was just a generic leader and the other was just there to be cute. Buttercup was my favorite because she is essentially the muscle of the trio and was the most "manlike" out of the three.

  • Brave
  • Strong
  • Chill

Raven

Raven is a character from the show Teen Titans. She has supernatural powers controlled by her emotions. I found her cool in general with her usually nonchalant and mature personality. I also liked how she could defeat most enemies if she wanted to because of her powers.

  • Powered by emotions
  • Laid back
  • Nonchalant
  • Mature

Mumen Rider

Mumen Rider is a hero from One Punch Man who, despite being just a regular human with a bicycle, achieved C-Class in the Hero Association. He is my favorite because his powers don't define him; his actions and heart do. He is selfless, brave, and honorable, and will do whatever he can to save a life. His presence alone is enough to make me laugh in a world full of people with unique abilities because he's just a guy with a bicycle and a strong heart.

  • Selfless
  • Brave
  • Honorable
  • Determined
